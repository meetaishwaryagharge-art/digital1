Our Story

From a small loft to a global studio.

HOW Studio was founded in 2015 by creative directors Maya Chen and James Harlow in a small East London loft. What started as a two-person operation quickly grew into one of the most respected design studios in Europe.

We've since expanded to a team of 24 creatives, strategists, and technologists — united by a shared obsession with exceptional craft and meaningful communication.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

01

Craft Over Speed

We believe the best work takes time. We never compromise quality for a deadline, and we're proud to work with clients who share that philosophy.

02

Purpose with Beauty

Aesthetic beauty and strategic purpose are not opposites — they're partners. Every design choice must earn its place by serving both the eye and the goal.

03

Enduring Over Trendy

Trends fade. We design for longevity — creating visual systems and brand identities that remain relevant and resonant years into the future.

How We Work

A refined process for extraordinary results.

01

Discovery & Immersion

We begin every project by listening. Through workshops, interviews, and immersive research, we develop a deep understanding of your brand, audience, and ambitions.

Weeks 1–2
02

Strategy & Direction

We synthesise research into a clear creative strategy — a brief that defines the direction, principles, and parameters for the creative work ahead.

Weeks 2–3
03

Exploration & Craft

Our designers explore multiple creative directions before refining the most promising into highly resolved, presentation-ready concepts.

Weeks 3–6
04

Refinement & Delivery

We refine the chosen direction with you, then prepare comprehensive deliverables — brand guidelines, digital assets, print files — ready for the world.
